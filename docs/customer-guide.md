# Customer Guide

Welcome to Satyam Printing Press. This guide outlines how customers interact with the platform.

## 1. Account Creation & Profile
- Users can browse the storefront without an account.
- To make a purchase, users must register via the **Sign Up** page.
- Once registered, the user profile allows customers to store multiple shipping/billing addresses and select a default address to expedite future checkouts.

## 2. Browsing & Customizing Products
- Customers can navigate through the store by category.
- On a Product Details page, customers are presented with dynamic customization fields specific to the printing product (e.g., selecting `A4` Size, `300 GSM` Paper, `Glossy` Lamination).
- **Design Upload**: Customers can upload their print-ready designs (PDF, PNG, JPG, SVG). Files are securely validated and attached to the cart payload.

## 3. Shopping Cart & Coupons
- The Shopping Cart automatically calculates subtotal, GST based on the product's tax bracket, and shipping fees (e.g., free shipping on orders above ₹2000).
- Customers can apply active promotional discount codes. The system validates minimum order values and expiry dates before recalculating the Grand Total.

## 4. Checkout & Payments
- Proceed to checkout to verify shipping details.
- Satyam Printing Press uses Razorpay for secure payments.
- Upon confirming the order, the Razorpay widget opens to process UPI, Credit/Debit cards, or Net Banking.
- Upon success, the system creates the order and sends a confirmation notification.

## 5. Order Tracking
- In the **My Orders** dashboard, customers can view their complete purchase history.
- Clicking on a specific order reveals the real-time fulfillment timeline (e.g., "Printing Started", "Shipped").
- Customers can download their official PDF invoice directly from this view.
